Holding itself in a perspective of potential energy, it lies in the waiting for the opportune moment to unfurl. Its ever-rising energy aligns most with the ascendant might of the dominant Yang. The challenge posed is to uphold a tempered state of alertness amidst the extensive stretches of delay.
