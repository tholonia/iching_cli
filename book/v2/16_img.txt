Tiffany’s stained glass technique, with its vibrant colors and interplay of light, emphasizes the Tholonic Model's core ideas of integration and emergence, where individual components contribute to a greater unified experience. Impressionism heightens the sensory engagement, reflecting the negotiation and balance of energies within the Thologram, while Tiffany’s mastery and symbolic approach elevate the work to award-winning quality, resonating with the universal love that guides creation and evolution.
