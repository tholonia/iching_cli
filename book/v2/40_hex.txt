This hexagram bestows upon the Tholon of transformation a distinguished attribute, the catalyst of a breakthrough. It promulgates an ethos of liberation established through the dismissal of inhibiting shackles. Its characteristic essence is the explosive vitality of dissipation, and its opportune detachment is that of materialization. Its triumph is a product of discerning fleeting moments of prospect with acuity. The energy that it exudes is an advancing, budding yang. The obstacle it must surmount lies in preserving equilibrium amidst the whirlwind of swift metamorphoses.
