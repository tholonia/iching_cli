This hexagram infuses the tholon of completion and renewal with the qualities of subtle penetration, presenting a pathway back to the origin. It offers a perspective shaped by the cumulative prowess of an unobtrusive yet constant influence. Its character unfolds as persistent, adaptable and vastly pervasive, quietly manifesting its gentle sway to foster steady progress. Success is achieved not through brute force, but through the gentle persistence of subtle pressure. The energy it exudes is a looming, dominant yang that gently descends. Nonetheless, it presents a challenge to persist without the exertion of force, a divine dance between tenacity and serenity.
