The subject of a tree represents steady evolution and the growth of civilizations, reflecting the foundational elements needed for sustainable development. This is symbolized through a fusion of African and Japanese cultural motifs, highlighting a balanced integration of diverse traditions that enhance community cohesion. The chosen mediums of wood and textiles evoke a sense of organic creativity and adaptability, mirroring the dynamic process of natural growth and the weaving together of cultural narratives. The blend of realism and impressionism captures the intricate beauty and fluidity of progress, embodying both concrete reality and the ethereal nature of evolving awareness. This artistic representation aligns with the concept of universal love as a creative force, integrating and harmonizing various elements into a cohesive and nurturing whole.
