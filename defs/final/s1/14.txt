The object, style, medium, and artist chosen to represent the hexagram of RADIANCE reflect the dynamic interplay of awareness and creation. The sun symbolizes vitality and the gathering of resources, essential for early development and growth, aligning with the hexagram’s theme. Impressionism, with its emphasis on light and color, mirrors the fluid, evolving nature of awareness in the Tholonic Model, where each brushstroke represents a tholon contributing to a larger pattern. The digital medium enhances this by blending traditional and modern techniques, suggesting limitless potential and connectivity. Vincent van Gogh's style captures the emotive power of universal love as a creative force, imbuing the scene with symbolic depth and intention. His capacity to convey profound emotion and symbolism resonates with the model’s concept of awareness as a fundamental, driving force behind all manifestations.
