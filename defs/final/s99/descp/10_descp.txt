This hexagram bestows the mindful qualities of progressive growth upon the Tholon, arrayed in its nascent stage, crafting its core structure. It provides a perspective characterized by restrained might, tempered by profound awareness. Its essence embodies a type of subdued force, while its methodical conduct resonates with taking tangible form. The challenge it beckons one to conquer is in the masterful withholding of control whilst possessing unyielding strength.
