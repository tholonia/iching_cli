This hexagram imparts the capacity to tame formidable forces to the Tholon of Challenges, a realm of examination and fortification. It offers a vision of dynamic energy confined and guided purposefully. It embodies a progressive essence, flourishing in disciplined growth, journeying toward expression. Triumph is procured through the patient gathering of energy. Its vitality is a dominant, descending force of Yang. The challenge that it must surmount is the cultivation of power while preserving control.
