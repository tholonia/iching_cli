The subject of "Potential" aligns with the underlying theme of balancing latent energy, mindful conduct, and disciplined restraint, reflecting how awareness and intention shape reality. This is visually embodied through the fusion of realistic and impressionistic styles, which convey both precise detail and emotional depth, illustrating the forces of balance and control. The combination of metal and digital media represents the integration of ancient and contemporary elements, symbolizing the timeless interplay between energy and restraint. By merging Chinese and European Renaissance influences, the artwork captures a fusion of philosophical and artistic traditions, emphasizing a universal, harmonious union. The choice of styles and materials embodies the interconnectedness and dynamic equilibrium inherent in the subject, illustrating how potential is realized through conscious awareness and control.
