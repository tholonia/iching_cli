The subject of Mountain, Earth, and Balance evokes a sense of stability and alignment, which aligns with the principles of awareness and intention creating harmony. These elements represent the grounding force of awareness manifesting in the physical form, where the mountain symbolizes enduring strength and the earth embodies grounding forces. The fusion of realism and romanticism in style captures not only the tangible and visible aspects but also the ideal and emotional essence, reflecting a harmonious blend of structure and creativity. Using stone as a medium accentuates permanence and resilience, while paper adds an element of transience and expression, together illustrating the balance between enduring truths and evolving ideas. The cultural fusion of Chinese and Roman elements highlights a dialogue between traditions that emphasize harmony and order, enriching the visual representation of equilibrium through a cross-cultural lens.
